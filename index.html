About

I have been coding on and off since 2010, and after going the 'self-taught' route, I started my formal education as a web developer at Noroff in the fall of 2020.

I enjoy exploring and mastering new technologies, but I believe that we should take no shortcuts if it's depriving someone of having access to the product. Writing semantically correct markup and making the product accessible is important to me.
